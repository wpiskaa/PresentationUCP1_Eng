The Problem

Background

Why Hemo Scan Ku?

Many medical facilities still manage blood stock manually — causing critical, life-threatening delays.

  • 🔴
    Supply Chain Gaps: Manual tracking causes delays during critical emergencies when seconds matter.
  • 💉
    Invasive Procedures: Traditional blood tests require frequent needle use, adding discomfort to patients already in pain.
  • ⚠️
    Stock Depletion: Without real-time alerts, rare blood types run out before staff even realize it.

How It Works

Technology

Non-Invasive Innovation Pipeline

Three layers of technology working seamlessly — from skin to screen.

🔦
Layer 1

Optical Sensing

Infrared (IR) sensors detect hemoglobin levels through the skin — completely needle-free.

→
📡
Layer 2

IoT Integration

Data transmits wirelessly to our cloud server for immediate analysis, storage, and distribution.

→
💻
Layer 3

Admin Dashboard

Managers view live stock levels and receive AI-driven demand predictions in real-time.
