Background

The Problem: Why Hemo Scan Ku?

In many medical facilities, blood stock management is still inefficient and often manual. This leads to critical issues:

  • Supply Chain Gaps: Delays in tracking blood availability during emergencies.
  • Invasive Procedures: Traditional tests require frequent needle use, which can be painful for patients.
  • Stock Depletion: Lack of real-time alerts leads to unexpected stockouts of rare blood types.

02

Functions

Smart Features & Innovation

Hemo Scan Ku provides a dual solution: a hardware scanner and a management dashboard.

📊
Real-Time Monitoring Instant dashboard updates for blood inventory across PMI and Hospitals.
🔔
Critical Alert System Automatically triggers notifications when stock falls below 3 bags.
🚀
Emergency Requests Digital request system to rapidly acquire blood from other medical units.
03

Technology

How it Works: Non-Invasive Innovation

🔦

Optical Sensing

Uses Infrared (IR) sensors to detect hemoglobin levels through the skin without needles.

📡

IoT Integration

Data is transmitted wirelessly to our cloud server for immediate analysis and storage.

💻

Admin Dashboard

Managers view live stock levels and receive AI-driven demand predictions.

04

Obstacles

The Journey: Challenges Faced

Developing a medical-grade management system involved several significant hurdles:

Database Sync

Ensuring data consistency between different hospital databases and PMI units in real-time.

Network Latency

Optimizing the application to perform flawlessly even in areas with unstable internet connections.

User Adoption

Designing a simple enough UI for medical staff who are used to manual paperwork.
